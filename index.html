Flights from Vancouver to Toronto: What's the Pattern?

Home in Vancouver, Study in Toronto

According to Statistics Canada, 1.3% to 1.93% of bachelor’s degree graduates of post-secondary institutions in Ontario are from British Columbia. This population partially represent post-secondary students whose home is in Vancouver, but study in Toronto.

Please wait for the visualization!

What seems to be the cheapest/most expensive time of the year in months?

Let's compare between the cheapeset and most expensive time of the year.

This is a line graph, comparing how the prices fluctuate between cheapest and most expensive prices. We have first manually collected the most expensive and cheapest one-way Vancouver-Toronto ticket prices for adult, economy class in 2026. We searched Google Flights and Skyscanner. We put them on Google Spreadsheet, where you can find our dataset.

Now let's see the data in weeks.

This is a bar graph.

We first filtered the dataset by the week of the month. Then, we took the average of each week.

Please note! This is just a sample bar graph.

Now let's see the data in a day of the week.

This is a calendar heatmap.

To identify the statistically cheapest day of the week, we created a calendar heatmap visualization to highlight the day with the lowest ticket price for each individual week. The lower the price, the lighter the colour of each day would be. From the calendar heatmap, we have tallied the frequency of each day of the week (Monday, Tuesday, etc.) being the designated cheapest day across a chosen period (e.g., a month or quarter). This frequency count allows us to conclude which day of the week statistically offers the lowest ticket prices.

Please note! This is just a sample heatmap from Rising Stack blog.
